Pokémon Yellow:
Which Starter?

A guide to Bulbasaur, Charmander & Squirtle

Meet the Starters

Bulbasaur

Grass / Poison

The balanced choice. Strong against the first two gyms (Brock & Misty) and learns status moves early.

Cerulean City — from Melanie

Charmander

Fire

The hard-mode pick. Weak against the first two gyms but becomes the powerhouse Charizard at Lv. 36.

Route 24 — from Damian

Squirtle

Water

The fan favorite. Beats Brock easily, gets Surf (HM03), and Blastoise tanks late-game fights.

Vermilion City — from Officer Jenny

Head-to-Head

Bulbasaur

Strengths
  • Super effective vs. Gym 1 (Brock) & Gym 2 (Misty)
  • Learns Leech Seed + Sleep Powder early
  • Resists Electric (Lt. Surge) & Grass types
  • Available earliest — Cerulean City
Weaknesses
  • Weak to Psychic (Sabrina) & Fire (Blaine)
  • Venusaur's movepool is limited late-game

Charmander

Strengths
  • Charizard is a beast — high Sp. Atk & Speed
  • Learns Slash, Flamethrower, Fire Blast
  • Destroys Erika (Grass gym) & Lorelei's Ice types
Weaknesses
  • Struggles badly vs. Brock & Misty early
  • Available latest — Route 24 (after Nugget Bridge)
  • Fire types are redundant with Pikachu's coverage

Pick Bulbasaur

It covers the most gym weaknesses early, learns the best utility moves, and is available first. Squirtle is a close second — but Pikachu already handles Water's strengths.
